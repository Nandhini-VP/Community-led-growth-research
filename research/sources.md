## Why Community-Led Growth?

Community-led growth (CLG) is a go-to-market strategy where a company's user community becomes the primary driver of acquisition, retention, and expansion. Unlike traditional sales-led or product-led growth, CLG turns customers into advocates who bring in more customers — making it one of the most cost-efficient and scalable growth strategies for B2B SaaS.

## Cross-Expert Patterns

- Community-led growth is most effective when built around identity (e.g., operators, builders) rather than product usage.
- Early-stage communities are manually driven (founder/operator-led), not automated.
- Community ROI is indirect and shows up in retention, referrals, and expansion — not immediate acquisition.
- Strong alignment between product and community teams is critical for CLG success.
